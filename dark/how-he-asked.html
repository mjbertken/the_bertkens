How He Asked

Michael knew he had to be sneaky with the proposal and boy did he surprise me.

On Monday of the proposal week, Michael told me he had to drive up to Iowa for a “work meeting.” Knowing that I would be looking at his location on my phone, he sat in his car for an hour downtown des moines. After this so called “meeting,” he stopped by my parents house with a beautiful bouquet of flowers and asked my parents for my hand in marriage.

A few days later I told Michael that the two of us should use our season tickets and go on a date to the Chiefs Thursday night pre-season game. He quickly shut that idea down. He told me he had a lot of work to do and that none of his friends were going to the game so it wouldn’t be fun. “Coincidentally,” our friend Rae texted me soon after and asked if i had a lab coat and stethoscope and wanted to be a last minute model for their photoshoot. What are the chances…. I have all of those things, I get paid to be a model, AND i am free thursday night. Rae frequently needed last minute models for her work at an ad agency so i was happy to be able to help out a friend and get to be a model. Right before Rae picked me up, michael came out into our family room wearing his chiefs jersey. His friends decided to go to the game after all and it would be too fun to pass up. Ohhh was I mad….

“We were supposed to go to the game together!?!”

“I am sorry but now you get to be a beautiful model. I am running late and they are outside waiting for me. Can we have this fight later?”

I had to put my frustrations aside and smile pretty for the beginning of my (hopeful) modeling career. I am very serious about my modeling so I brought textbooks and other props to really convince people I was a student. After Rae had me fill out a W-2, she said I could leave the textbooks in the car and we could grab them later if we needed to. She took a few pictures of me in my lab coat and then a few without. She told me where to stand and in the last picture she had me look up and to the right. Then I felt this loving kiss on my cheek. When I turned and saw Michael standing there with the sweetest smile I finally knew what was really happening. He asked, “Do you really think I would go to the Chiefs game without you?” Then he got down on one knee and asked the most important question of my life. Through tears we hugged and kissed and then started laughing when we saw our family running towards us. They were all in on the surprise and were hiding during the proposal. They greeted us with champagne and we celebrated with a picnic on the lawn of liberty memorial. Once the sun set, Michael and I went out to dinner and then met up with his friends at blue line. They greeted us with cheese curds and beers. Between the love of our family and friends, the beautiful night, cheese curds and my FIANCE, it was the best night and surprise of my life :)
